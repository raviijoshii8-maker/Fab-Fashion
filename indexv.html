why Fab Fashion? slow approach

Clothing is the architecture of the everyday. In 2023 we opened a small shop in Minsk with a simple belief: buy less, choose well, make it last. Our pieces are sourced from European small-batch producers (Portugal, Poland, Ukraine) and a growing number of Belarusian sewists. Linen, organic cotton, Tencel — Fab Fashions that breathe and age gracefully.

We don't follow fast‑fashion cycles. Instead, we offer a permanent collection of essentials: shirts, trousers, dresses, jackets — updated slowly, repaired gladly. Every item is hand‑checked before hanging. The shop itself is a calm space; you can browse without music, try without rush. If something needs adjustment, our tailor is nearby.

materials we love: linen, wool, cupro, ramie

why natural fibres? — short guide

  • Linen (flax): grown locally (Belarus is a flax country!), gets softer with each wash, highly breathable, biodegradable.
  • Organic cotton: no pesticides, gentle on skin. We use only GOTS‑certified for cotton items.
  • Tencel (lyocell): made from eucalyptus pulp, closed‑loop process, silky drape.
  • Wool (merino / lambswool): renewable, temperature regulating. Our knits are mulesing‑free.
  • Cupro: made from cotton linter waste — smooth as silk, with less environmental impact.

We avoid virgin synthetics. Some blends use recycled polyester for durability, always labelled clearly.

five ways we differ

  1. Personal sizing appointment — book 30 min, we adjust lengths and take orders for tall/petite.
  2. Free mending for life — any item bought at Fab Fashion can be repaired (small fee for major work).
  3. No seasonal sales — instead we lower prices gradually on last pieces, never fake discounts.
  4. Local makers shelf — socks, scarves, ceramics from Belarusian artisans, with full transparency.
  5. Secondhand curation — we accept clean, well‑kept men’s shirts and jeans; swap or consign.
